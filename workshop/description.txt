[h1]Space Crusade – Digital Tabletop Adaptation[/h1]

Step into the grimdark universe of [b]Space Crusade[/b], now reimagined for [b]Tabletop Simulator[/b]. Command squads of fearless [b]Space Marines[/b] - choose from the stalwart [b]Blood Angels[/b], disciplined [b]Ultramarines[/b], or resilient [b]Imperial Fists[/b] - and launch tactical assaults aboard an alien-infested [b]space hulk[/b].

Fierce creatures like [b]Orks[/b], [b]Genestealers[/b], [b]Chaos Marines[/b], and [b]Androids[/b] stand in your way, while one player takes on the role of the alien adversary, thwarting the mission with cunning strategy and relentless pressure.

[h2]=== GAME FEATURES ===[/h2]

[list]
[*][b]Mission-based gameplay[/b] – Faithfully recreates the modular board and dynamic scenarios of the original game.
[*] Both game expansions [b]Mission: Dreadnought[/b] and[b]Eldar Attack[/b].
[*]Additional units from [i]White Dwarf[/i] magazine – [b]Scouts[/b], [b]Terminators[/b], [b]Librarians[/b] and [b]Tyranids and Hybrids[/b] add more tactical depth.
[*][b]Three playmats[/b] – Space Marines, Orks, and Eldars, allowing varied and thematic battles.
[*]Classic miniatures and components brought to life in digital form, enhancing tactical immersion and fast-paced gameplay.
[/list]

Bring your campaign to life and relive the classic cooperative and strategic thrills. Are you ready to reclaim the hulk... or will the alien forces crush the crusade?

[h2]=== INCLUDED MISSIONS ===[/h2]

All missions are pre-setup and stored in a [b]Memory Bag[/b] for quick one-click placement.

[list]
[*][b]12 base missions[/b]
[*][b]3 missions from the expansion "Mission: Dreadnought"[/b].
[*][b]4 missions from the expansion "Eldar Attack"[/b].
[*][b]New Mission Kit[/b] to easily create new missions.
[/list]

[h2]=== TABLETOP FEATURES ===[/h2]

[list]
[*][b]Move counters[/b] to show how far your units travelled.
[*][b]Automatic dice rollers[/b] for quick game play.
[*][b]Camera display[/b] which provides a mini-map on each player's console.
[/list]
